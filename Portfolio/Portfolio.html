Menu App

The Menu App is a user-friendly application designed to provide users with a seamless experience in exploring a menu that includes item ID, item price, item name, and item description.

Features:

  • Menu Display: Users can easily view the menu items presented in a structured layout, making it convenient to browse through different options.
  • Detailed Information: Each menu item includes essential details such as item ID, price, name, and a brief description, allowing users to make informed decisions.

Encountered Challenge: One notable challenge during the development process was encountering errors related to the menuData structure. Initially, the data was not recognized as an array, resulting in unexpected behavior within the application. However, through careful debugging and restructuring of the data, this issue was successfully resolved, ensuring the smooth functioning of the app.
